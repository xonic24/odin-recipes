Tavern Bread Recipe

Description

Easy to make tasty Viking bread

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of unbleached flour
  • 2 cups of whole wheat flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1 to 2 cups of buttermilk
  • 1 cup of cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup of summer sausage

Instructions

  1. Step

    2. Preheat oven to 450 degrees.

  2. Step

    3. Sift the flours together with salt and baking powder in a mixing bowl.

  3. Step

    4. Add the meat and cheese and mix together.

  4. Step

    5. Make a well in the flour and add buttermilk slowly while mixing.

  5. Step

    6. Make sure that the dough is moist pat into a loaf and place into an oiled cast iron skillet.

  6. Step

    7. Bake for 10 minutes or until the bread has risen, hen lower heat to 375 degrees and bake for another 30 to 35 minutes or until it's done.
